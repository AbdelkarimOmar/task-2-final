effects of reducing screen time prove to be life changing

people of australia share their experiences of self-improvement by limiting extended daily screen time

Australians are increasingly reporting lifestyle improvements after cutting extended daily screen time. Many say the change has led to better sleep, stronger relationships, and more productive use of their free hours.

Experts suggest that reducing screen dependency can also help lower stress levels and improve mental health. Social groups and schools are encouraging challenges that promote limited screen use, showing that even small changes can have lasting positive effects on overall wellbeing.
